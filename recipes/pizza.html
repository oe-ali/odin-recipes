Pizza


Description

Pizza is a dish that uses kneaded dough that is then shaped into a flat bread. Afterwards a tomato sauce
is spread over the bread, then come the various toppings which vary between mushrooms, onion slices, pepperoni
slices, grated cheese, mozzarella, etc, and in the end the pizza is being baked in the oven.

The origins of this dish trace back to Italy which got popularized thanks to American cooks.
