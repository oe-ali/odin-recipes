Turkish Pide Bread


Description

Pide is a type of flat bread that carries its origins in Turkey. Essentially, this is
a staple of the Turkish breakfast cuisine. This bread is topped with sesame seeds.

There's also a version of pide that looks like a boat which is semi-open so one can see which filling
has been used (either white-cheese-filling, ground beef or mashed potatoes). We will focus on the plain
Turkish Flat Bread, though.


Ingredients

  • Warm milk (wholemilk or 2%)
  • Instant dry yeast
  • Eggs
  • All purpose flour (4 1/2 cups, not more)
  • Yogurt
