Lasagna


Description

Lasagna, or Lasagne in the Italian language, is a pasta dish that is
made up of several layers of very flat and wide pasta sheets.

In Italian cuisine the layers can either contain tomato sauce with grounded beef, b√©chamel sauce, vegetables,
various cheese types and other seasonings and spices. Before the lasagna is put in the oven grated cheese may
be sprinkled on top.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound ofsweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of canned tomato sauce
  • 2 of cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • about 450g of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • about 500g of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cups of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare sauce

    • Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a pan over medium heat until well browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.
    • Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds,
      and pepper.
    • Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

  2. Prepare the lasagna

    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    • Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
    • Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

  3. Mix the cheese

    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and
      1/2 teaspoon salt.

  4. Assemble all things from previous steps

    • To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly.
    • Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture.
    • Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    • Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
    • Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    • Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the
      foil does not touch the cheese.

  5. The finishing step

    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
    • Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
